Containers

Containers are meant to hold centralized content, like a blog post or a login box. Containers are also responsive.

Note: Containers have the same size on smaller screens. Depending on the parent element (if smaller than the container), it will have a size of 100% minus a margin of 16px on both sides.

chevron_right
container-smaller

The container-smaller class has a width of 420px, ideal for login boxes or simple forms in general.
